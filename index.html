Common AI Legal Risks

Many organizations adopt AI quickly without understanding the legal and operational risks. These are the issues we help businesses identify and manage.

Confidentiality Risk

Employees may enter sensitive business information, contracts, customer data, or trade secrets into AI platforms.

Privacy Violations

AI tools may create risk when personal information is collected, processed, transferred, or used without proper safeguards.

Bias & Discrimination

AI systems may produce unfair outcomes in hiring, lending, housing, insurance, pricing, or customer service.

Inaccurate Outputs

AI can generate incorrect information that looks reliable, creating business, reputational, or legal exposure.

IP Ownership Issues

AI-generated content may raise questions about copyright, ownership, training data, licensing, and commercial use.

Weak Vendor Terms

AI vendor contracts may contain unfavorable clauses about data use, liability, warranties, confidentiality, or termination.
