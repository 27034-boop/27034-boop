About Law for AI

Law for AI is focused on the legal, regulatory, and commercial challenges created by artificial intelligence. We support startups, law firms, investors, and established companies that want to use AI responsibly while protecting their business, customers, data, and intellectual property.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need legal advice if my company only uses third-party AI tools?

Yes. Even if you do not build your own model, your company can still face legal risk related to privacy, contracts, data handling, output misuse, confidentiality, and customer disclosures.

Can you help create an internal AI policy for employees?

Yes. We can help define which tools are approved, what data may be entered, what review is required, and how oversight should work.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. We support founders and emerging companies that need focused guidance without unnecessary complexity.

Can you review AI vendor contracts?

Yes. We review AI vendor terms, procurement agreements, licensing provisions, data use clauses, and risk allocation language.
